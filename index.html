Cyber Eye is an indie company founded by one person.


We have 5 years of self-training and development in the direction of game development behind our shoulders.


We started with small projects for Android and Ios and eventually settled on the PC platform. At the moment the company consists of several people. Each of us is passionate about our work. We are focused on the further development of our projects and the creation of new ones so that each of you can find a game to your liking. We also strive to expand our staff to release updates, expansions and new games with the least possible delay.


Subscribe to our channels official pages and the channel in the discord so as not to miss the latest and most interesting news.
